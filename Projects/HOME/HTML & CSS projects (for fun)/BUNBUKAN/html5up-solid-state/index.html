Foreword

The school is run as a non-profit association affiliated to the Institute of Classical Japanese Budo Culture which was formed by Shihan Chris Rowen after the passing of late Hanshi Gogen Yamaguichi. Bunbukan was formed in the early 1990s to preserve and pass on certain elements of the wonderful art of Goju Ryu Karate-Do and related aspects.

Goju Ryu Style of Karate

Traditional karate originates from the island of Okinawa. Originally, only three styles existed, each of which was named after the city from which it first started. These are: Tomari-Te, Shuri-Te and Naha-Te.

Dojo Etiquette

what is karate?

The Japanese term "Kara" means empty and "te" means hand. Therefore, Karate translates as "empty hand", the art of self-defence without a weapon.

What is the meaning of BUNBUKAN?

The meaning of Bunbukan lies within three Japanese characters. BUN can be interpreted as mind, study, education, literacy, arts and introspection. BU is the prefix to Budo that represents the way of martial arts; but the deeper meaning gleaned from the ideogram BU is to stop fighting and conflict and it is this aspect that we pursue in Bunbukan. KAN denotes the physical place where the mind and body can be joined in this particular practice.
