FOREWORD

The school is run as a non-profit association affiliated to the Institute of Classical Japanese Budo Culture which was formed by Shihan Chris Rowen after the passing of late Hanshi Gogen Yamaguichi. Bunbukan was formed in the early 1990s to preserve and pass on certain elements of the wonderful art of Goju Ryu Karate-Do and related aspects.

The Japanese term "Bunbu" is a concept used to describe the essential and ultimate balance required within arts and 'kan' refers to a place. So 'Bunbukan' translates as a place where the nature of 'bunbu' is sought, practised, studied and preserved. The institute's main mission is to promote budo culture to anyone who has an interest in traditional martial arts. The prime philosophy of the school is to disseminate knowledge, so it can be fostered and shared with anyone who wants to understand the essence of martial art.

In this day, with all the changes that are happening around us, it is part of our philosophy to pass the right values to our students, and to ensure that it makes a positive contribution in their development. At the institution of Classical Japanese Budo Culture, our core values are to ensure that people of all walks of life are given the chance to to practice the art within a positive environment. We should never forget the past as it forms part of our present and it is for this reason that at the Bunbukan, we lay great empashis on the classical values of art.

At the Bunbukan, we try to cultivate the ideal human nature in a physical and spiritual manner, by training the body, the mind and the soul. Our aim is to forge human spirit and make our students become better person, not only as somebody who can deffend himself or herself against physical violence, but most of all to prepare them to cope with the challenges of this modern world. Heighten thei virtue, master the stategy of winning without fighting and seek the ultimate secret.

The school is headed by Sensei Danny Sanhye, 5th Dan Goju Ryu. He is a senior instructor of Bunbukan Association and a branch chief. He has been training in Goju Ryu Karate System for almost four decades. He has been privileged of being taught by two top students of Late Hanshi Gogen Yamaguichi. He received instruction from the karate master Shihan Noor Dullo and Shihan Chris Rowen, both direct descendents of the founder of Goju Ryu Karate - Master Gogen Yamaguichi. Sensei Danny holds an international licence from International Traditional Goju Ryu Karate Do and an advanced teaching diploma with merit in yoga from India. He is fellow chartered accountant and also holds an advanced degree in International Law.
